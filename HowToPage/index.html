Baked Red-Pepper Penne

Prep + Cook Time: 2 Hours

Baked Red-Pepper Penne


Prep + Cook Time: 2 hours

Ingredients

Serves: 4-6 people

  • 4 small shallots
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1.5lbs of red peppers
  • 1.5lbs of fresh tomatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Large sprig of fresh basil
  • 16oz of penne
  • 1 1/2 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese (for serving)
