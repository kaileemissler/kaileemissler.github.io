BIO




Flume is an electronica DJ, musician, and record producer from Sydney, Australia. He began composing music at the age of 11 and his first album self-titled Flume debuted in 2012. He begins a worldwide tour this year and is regarded as a pioneer of the future bass genre. Flume has remixed songs from popular pop artists such as Lorde and Sam Smith and his 2016 album Skin won the Best Dance/Electronic Album at the 2017 Grammy Awards.
